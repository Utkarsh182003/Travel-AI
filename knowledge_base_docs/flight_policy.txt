Important Flight Booking Policies:
- All international flights require booking at least 7 days in advance.
- Economy class is the default for flights under 4 hours.
- Business class is allowed for flights longer than 4 hours or for trips over $1000.
- Baggage allowance is 20kg for economy and 30kg for business class.
- Flight cancellations must be requested 24 hours prior to departure for a full refund.
